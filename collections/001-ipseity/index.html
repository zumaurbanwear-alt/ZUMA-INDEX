01 — Overview What this entry is

OVERVIEW.

IPSEITY — two entries, each documenting a distinct approach to the same axiom: identity persists independent of observation.

Classification: Garment. Origin: Casablanca. Status: Verified.

02 — Concept The question behind it

CONCEPT.

IPSEITY asks one question: what remains when no one is looking? Both entries document the same answer from opposite directions. What is stated stays minimal. What is embroidered, printed, or placed inside the garment carries the rest. Identity is not a declaration. It is a trace left behind, observed or not.

03 — Research Where it started

RESEARCH.

The research began with a question, not a sketch: what constitutes identity in the absence of a witness? The chosen term — ipseity, from the Latin ipse, self — was picked for precision over sound. The field work followed two threads: objects worn without thought, and marks left unintentionally. The two pieces in this drop are the documented result of that research, not its illustration.
